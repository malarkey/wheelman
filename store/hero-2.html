Made of high-quality chrome vanadium steel, with heat treatment, high hardness, good toughness, wear resistant, corrosion resistant, strong and durable. This screwdriver set includes six sizes.

Details

Magnetic tips

Each screwdriver has a strong magnetic tip, easy to lift and fix the screw, effectively preventing the screw from falling and losing. Magnetism does not weaken easily.

Easy to use

Ergonomic anti-slip particles and soft handle, providing a soft and comfortable feel while working. Dimensions are printed on the end of the screwdriver for easy identification.

Wide applications

Perfect for all types of break-ins, including burglaries, disabling alarm systems, door opening, lock box opening, safe cracking, stealing cars, and general criminal activities.

Various sizes

The screwdriver set includes 6 sizes: 6*38mm, 3*75mm, 4*75mm, 5*100mm, 6*125mm, and 7*150mm. Each size has cross head screwdriver to meet different needs.
